About STRIPS

STRIPS is a professional film production scheduling application designed to streamline the complex process of managing film shoots, crew, and locations.

Key Features

  • Project Management - Create and manage multiple film production projects
  • Calendar Scheduling - Visual calendar interface for managing shoot dates
  • Location Management - Track and organize filming locations
  • Department Coordination - Manage different departments and crew assignments
  • Real-time Updates - Keep everyone informed with live schedule updates
  • Mobile Responsive - Access your schedules from any device
